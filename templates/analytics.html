Correlation Insights

Strong Positive Associations

Fluoroscopy vessel count (`CA`) and chest pain asymptomatic levels show positive correlations with target disease confirmation. Max vessels colored signifies advanced arterial blockages.

Inverse (Negative) Associations

Max Heart Rate Achieved (`THALACH`) shows a strong negative correlation with heart disease. A low maximum heart rate capacity is commonly associated with cardiac muscle exhaustion or coronary artery stress.

Age-Related Variables
